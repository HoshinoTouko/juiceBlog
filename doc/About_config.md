# About config.json  

## Global  

### title & intro  

The title and introduction, but not too long.  

### url  

The blog's home url.  

### plugins  

The plugins included in the blog. The plugins must be put in app/Plugin.  

### posts  

The blog's posts source.  
source@parse://name or url  

#### example:  

- local@md://post1
- local@html://post2
- remote@md://https://gayhub.com/
